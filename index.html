Jester's Litany

Rot’s child walks the halls of power. Rocky fell, heir.
Winds or family whispers? Astarte’s family schemes quietly.
Dupe on family? Dupe on’t! More gun family to war.

Build de Berg rises. Free Mason is, are we?
Three literal Co-missioners plot, Cowardly Feigning Rats pretend.
Tryna Illuminate me? Wasted Eyes Fade and Rome-o’s Clubs plot.

Bohemian’s Grave is in hollow nights, Skull and Bones stare silently.
Commit 300 Tea parties, Nights in city Of Malta hides secrets O’ pus, O’ day.
Rose of Crucians blooms where Nights’ Temple Lure.

Black Hand leaves red stains, Scroll and Key in hidden books.
Wolf’s Head watches Torch of Honor burn, St. Elmo dances in the streets.
Elizabeth in club gatherings, Books and Snakes twist truth.

The Spade Engraves, Try Dent Society.
Herr Met Tic at the Order of the Golden Dawn.
You UNite? or UNdo? I question WHO or IM Fooled and World’s Bank stores secrets.

NAy-TO them! EW EW EU! Gates to Bill and Melinda’s Foundation shine.
George’s Obtuse Sore Feet ugh, your Business Is Sin.
Fed all, Reserved for few. Enjoy, Comply, Behave.

Goggles your googly eyes. Eve’s Apple, Adam's bite.
Micro-soft lies, A maze on twists paths.
Meta ain’t great, Big Farmer plants seeds that never grow.
Tick-Tock, the mind-clock, BRAIN-ROT!

You’re Tube’d! Face the Book! I like grahams, not insta!
Twit-t’er flaps, X THE BIRD!
Hollow woods not holly nor jolly.
See I, Aye! Black Rock knows, Vain Guard waits, Count Sell: 13!

Never Winning Overlords.
I jest, I see, I write, I spill—the world stands still.
